Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Time: _
  • Serves: _
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast: 1-piece
Lettuce : 1 head
tomatoes: 2 medium
cucumbers: 1 large
Olive oil: 2 tbsp
balsamic vinegar: 1 tbsp

Step 1: Grill the chicken breast until cooked through..

Step 2: Chop the lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumbers

Step 3:Slice the grilled chicken and add it to the salad.

Step 4: Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, toss, and serve.
